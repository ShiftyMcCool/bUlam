Whether you are here to learn bUlam, or are just here to look around, the best place to start is in the alphabet section.
This will introduce you to the glyphs that make up bUlam. You will learn how each one is pronounced as well as the key on your keyboard that will create the symbol while using the font in other programs.

After you learn how to pronounce the glyphs, you are free to enter any of the other sections. However, the links above have been ordered from top to bottom in the easiest progression to learn the language.
